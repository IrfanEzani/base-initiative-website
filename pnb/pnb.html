PNB Scholarship

The PNB scholarship programmes provide opportunities for students with outstanding academic achievement to pursue tertiary education in public universities, selected private universities as well as top universities abroad.

PNB in a Nutshell

Permodalan Nasional Berhad (PNB) is Malaysia’s leading fund management company with assets under management of over RM279 billion. PNB’s portfolio covers strategic investments in leading Malaysian corporates, global equity, private investments and real estate. .

All You Need to Know: PNB

  • Economics, Law, Accounting and Finance, Information Technology, Property and Hospitality, Actuarial Science, Risk Management, Philosophy, politics and economics (PPE)
  • EXCEPT medicine, dentistry and courses NOT related to PNB
  • Minimum 8As (A and A+ only) in SPM 2019
  • Minimum 2A for Bahasa Inggeris 1119 (GCE-O) SPM 2019
  • Not exceeding 19 years of age on 1 January 2020
    Strong leadership qualities, high teamwork capabilities, excel in both extra-curricular activities and sports
  • No immediate family has ever been or is receiving any Scholarship Awards from PNB
  • Has excellent skills and communication skills
  • Certificates of Participation / Achievement
  • SPM/IGCSE Transcript
  • Scanned Identification Card
  • Curriculum Vitae
  • Parents’ Salary Slip of EA form
  • Passport-size photo
  • School Leaving Certificate
*All must be Certified True Copy
Application can be made by using PNB “Scholarship Application Form” and to be sent to email: edd@pnb.com.my
  • Email your application form to edd@pnb.com.my
  • Stage 1: Online : Critical Reasoning Test Battery (CRTB) assessment
  • Stage 2: Online : Interview Session
  • Stage 3: Online : Occupational Personality Questionnaire (OPQ) session
  • Stage 4: Online : Group Presentation (Debate)
  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why do you want to pursue this course?
  • Questions on current issues
